What to Expect During a Newborn Photography Session

A typical newborn photography session can last anywhere from two to four hours. This allows plenty of time for feeding, soothing, and posing the baby. Photographers often recommend scheduling the session within the first two weeks of birth when the baby is still very sleepy and can be easily posed.

Preparation Tips for Parents

Schedule Early: Book your session during your pregnancy to ensure availability.

Keep the Baby Awake: Try to keep the baby awake for an hour or two before the session to ensure they are sleepy during the shoot.

Feeding: Feed the baby right before the session to keep them content and sleepy.
